## 🧭 About Me

I'm a 17-year-old self-driven student passionate about **cybersecurity** and **applied AI** — with a specific focus on making AI accessible in **resource-constrained and geopolitically unstable environments**.

While most AI is built for the cloud and the privileged, I build for the edges — the farmer on 2G, the rural driver without a smartphone, the community with no reliable internet. I believe resilience, privacy, and accessibility should be first-class features of every AI system.

> *"AI should work for the farmer with a 2G connection as much as it works for the developer with a MacBook."*

---

## 🔭 What I'm Building

### 🤖 LocalLLM Voice Box
> *Turning old Android phones into offline AI assistants*

A GUI desktop/mobile app that runs a **local LLM entirely on-device** — targeting phones with 4GB+ RAM. Think Alexa or Google Home, but with **zero internet dependency**. Designed for resilience in regions where connectivity is unreliable or where digital surveillance is a concern.

- 🧠 Powered by **Ollama / llama.cpp**
- 📵 Fully **offline** — no cloud, no data leakage
- ♻️ Breathes new life into low-cost, aging hardware
- 🌍 Critical for geopolitically unstable or rural regions

---

### 🛺 AI E-Rickshaw Booking Agent *(Rural India)*
> *AI dispatch — no app, no internet, no literacy barrier*

A **call-based AI dispatch system** for rural e-rickshaw networks. A customer dials a number, an AI voice agent collects pickup/drop details, and automatically connects them to the nearest available driver — all over a standard phone call.

- 📞 Works on **any phone** — no smartphone, no app, no data
- 🗣️ Fully voice-driven — removes literacy barriers entirely
- 🔁 End-to-end **agentic AI workflow** for real-world logistics
- 🤝 Serves communities often ignored by mainstream tech
